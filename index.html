ABOUT THE SYMPOSIUM- ​ 

         The symposium aims to bring together educators and researchers to explore Problem-Based Learning (PBL) practices in India. PBL places students as active problem solvers, engaging them in real world scenarios that demand critical thinking, collaboration, and application of knowledge. We welcome your contributions to share experiences related to PBL within the following tracks with theme: “PBL for Interdisciplinary learning’’
