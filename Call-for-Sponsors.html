Call For Sponsors

    Problem Based and Project Based Learning (PBL) are important practices of student engagement in education all over the world. These two approaches have made significant inroads into engineering education as well. Recognizing the importance of PBL, Anurag University is hosting Regional Research Symposium on PBL during December 19-21, 2024. This Symposium is being organized in collaboration with UNESCO Centre for PBL in Engineering Science and Sustainability located at Aalborg University, Denmark. Indo-Universal Collaboration for Engineering Education is partnering with us. We expect participation from academic institutions in the Asia Pacific Region (predominantly from India) who are practicing and wanting to practice PBL.

      The symposium welcomes industry participation at all levels, which includes the technical program, especially workshops and vendor exhibition. Details about these and the symposium are available at the conference website:  www.rrspbl.co.in

       Various sponsorship opportunities are available as listed below. Sponsors are encouraged to participate in defining the industry, research, and user symposium events. Companies and R&D laboratories are encouraged to present their exhibits at the conference.
